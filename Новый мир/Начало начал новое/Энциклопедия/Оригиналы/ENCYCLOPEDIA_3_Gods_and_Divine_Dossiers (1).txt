WORLD LORE ENCYCLOPEDIA — PRE-EARTHFALL (PF2)
VOLUME III: GODS + DIVINE DOSSIERS (NO MAPS)
Version: 2026-02-21

====================================================================
HOW TO READ THIS VOLUME
====================================================================
Each deity entry includes:
- ROLE (what reality function they stabilize)
- ORIGIN (top-down seat vs bottom-up emergence)
- METAPHYSICS (how they feed on anima; what they can/can’t do)
- PROXIES (how they act without “coming down personally”)
- RELATIONSHIPS (front interactions)
- CULT TONES (how worship feels)
- GM HOOKS (campaign use)

Remember:
[CANON ✅] Spark/anima conservation: gods cannot create infinite power.
They are engines of flow, not printers of existence.

====================================================================
A) COSMIC-LEVEL FORCES
====================================================================

1) AOS — THE EXPERIMENTER
ROLE: Origin of Spark; designer of world-iterations.
ORIGIN: primordial creator outside local pantheon politics.
METAPHYSICS: rarely acts directly; intervention risks destabilizing the experiment.
PROXIES: Jhergal, occasional “archival artifacts” from other worlds.
RELATIONSHIPS: opposed by Thanatos; protected by Jhergal protocol.
CULT TONE: philosophy, research, humility before unknown laws.
GM HOOKS: an artifact from a failed world breaks local causality; a cult tries to “rewrite” Spark.

2) JHERGAL — PROTOCOL ARCHIVE
ROLE: Stabilize causality and procedure; keep records of reality.
ORIGIN: first Spark-bearer; Aos’s prime creation.
METAPHYSICS: power expressed as rules/records, not emotional miracles.
PROXIES: Ions (rare), archivist orders, seal-keepers.
CULT TONE: austere; scribes, judges, notaries; taboo against falsifying records.
GM HOOKS: a stolen protocol tablet lets Thanat edit a contract of reality.

3) THANATOS / THANAT — ENTROPY
ROLE: Dissolution; turning structure to noise.
ORIGIN: cosmic sibling force; Thanat is avatar inside world.
METAPHYSICS: rarely “creates.” He accelerates collapse by exploiting existing cracks.
PROXIES: inevitability sects, saboteurs, corrupted seers, disaster-cults.
CULT TONE: quiet cynicism; “meaning is a lie; end is honest.”
GM HOOKS: a peace treaty fails for no clear reason; later PCs find a single altered clause.

4) ASTRA / BOMBADIL (OPTIONAL TOOL)
ROLE: Spectator chaos; narrative wildcard.
ORIGIN: sibling who enjoys the show; Bombadil avatar.
METAPHYSICS: small pushes, coincidence, irony.
PROXIES: trickster cults, wandering “luck events.”
CULT TONE: laughter that can kill.
GM HOOKS: Astra rescues PCs… but now they owe a “joke” to the world.

====================================================================
B) SYSTEM ADMINISTRATION AND PLANAR ARCHITECTURE
====================================================================

5) PHARASMA — ADMINISTRATOR OF SPARK/CYCLE
ROLE: Continuity of souls; stable traffic; prevent systemic collapse.
ORIGIN: assigned administrator by Aos/Jhergal framework.
METAPHYSICS: can direct/ignite/assign Spark but cannot multiply infinite Spark.
PROXIES: funerary orders; cycle judges; seam engineers (Jotunborn).
CULT TONE: solemn practicality; taboo-driven; “don’t break the cycle.”
GM HOOKS: Pharasma’s agent demands PCs close a seam even if it kills a town.

6) IHYS — THE FREEDOM ARCHITECT (DEAD, BUT RESIDUE LIVES)
ROLE: Freedom as meaning of Spark.
ORIGIN: created by Pharasma as architect; later destroyed by Asmodeus.
METAPHYSICS: no active church; residue persists as aboleth blessing.
PROXIES: aboleths; secret emancipator cults; freedom philosophies.
CULT TONE: forbidden idealism; “choice above safety.”
GM HOOKS: Ihysite rebels want to reignite freedom in elementals—risking new wars.

7) ASMODEUS — ORDER AND CONTRACT
ROLE: Prevent collapse by controlling will through law.
ORIGIN: created architect; becomes coup victor and systemic tyrant.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on contracts, obedience, and the divine tithe of lawful worship.
PROXIES: priests of law; seal bureaucracies; infernal contractors; Thassilon institutions.
CULT TONE: intimidating efficiency; salvation by paperwork.
GM HOOKS: Asmodeus offers to stop a drought—if a city signs away a generation’s freedom.

8) ABADAR — MEASURE, CITY, EXCHANGE
ROLE: Standardize civilization; make contracts stable and productive.
ORIGIN: administrative seat in pantheon formation.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on exchange, trade trust, lawful infrastructure.
PROXIES: banks, guilds, magistrates, engineers.
CULT TONE: practical prosperity; “a bridge is a prayer.”
GM HOOKS: Abadar-backed merchants try to sign a “river rights” treaty with Gozreh druids.

9) DESNA — DREAMS, ETHER, PATH
ROLE: connective tissue between planes; dreams as navigation.
ORIGIN: seat emerging early; not required to be good at first.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on hope, travel, dream-rituals; influences coincidences.
PROXIES: dream seers, travelers, fey proto-ecology.
CULT TONE: wanderlust, prophecy, gentle danger.
GM HOOKS: Desna warns PCs via shared dream; ignoring it strengthens Thanat’s inevitability.

====================================================================
C) MATERIAL PLANE SHAPERS
====================================================================

10) ROVAGUG — CREATOR TURNED SABOTEUR
ROLE: Material form engine; later: destabilizer through neglect.
ORIGIN: major creator force in pantheon distribution.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on primal creation drive and later on rage/resentment flows.
PROXIES: monstrous cults; “first hunt” traditions; sabotage spirits; apex beasts.
CULT TONE: awe and dread; not always evil; “the world is teeth.”
GM HOOKS: Rovagug cult offers to close a seam by feeding it a city.

11) SHELYN — HARMONY OF FORM (CREATION OF ROVAGUG)
ROLE: culture and beauty as stabilizing structure.
ORIGIN: created by Rovagug as assistant.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on art discipline; can stabilize minds and cities.
PROXIES: architects, artisans, choirs, temple-workshops.
CULT TONE: demanding эстетика; “beauty is law.”
GM HOOKS: Shelyn’s order wants to rebuild a ruined city—but imposes harsh caste rules.

12) DOU-BRAL — DEPTH AND SHADOW (CREATION OF ROVAGUG)
ROLE: hidden truth; shadow as necessary half.
ORIGIN: created by Rovagug as assistant.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on confession, taboo knowledge, grief, endurance.
PROXIES: confessors, shadow-judges, forbidden archivists.
CULT TONE: quiet intensity; “look at what you hide.”
GM HOOKS: Dou-Bral cult offers to reveal a Thanat agent if PCs sacrifice reputation.

13) SARANRAE — MERCY AND RESCUE
ROLE: healing, rescue, light against despair.
ORIGIN: pantheon seat; grows through mortal suffering and compassion flows.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on mercy and sacrifice; conflicts with nature cruelty post-Gozreh.
PROXIES: healers, hospitallers, dawn knights.
CULT TONE: warmth with steel; mercy that demands courage.
GM HOOKS: Sarenrae priests evacuate a city; Asmodeus wants to keep citizens as “contract property.”

14) TORAG — THE HOLD AND THE FORGE (ELEVATED HERO POSSIBILITY)
ROLE: defend craft and community; hold stability.
ORIGIN: can be elevated greatest dwarf (non-primordial).
METAPHYSICS: feeds on oaths, craft, defense labor.
PROXIES: smith guilds, hold guardians, oath rings.
CULT TONE: stern protection; forgiveness is expensive.
GM HOOKS: Torag’s faithful demand punishment for duergar—risking civil war.

====================================================================
D) DRACONIC SEAT
====================================================================

15) APSU — GUIDING PACT
ROLE: regulate dragon violence; keep pact.
ORIGIN: elevated from dragons into divine seat.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on oathkeeping, restraint, protection.
PROXIES: White Cloaks (救援 orders), pact-kobolds.
CULT TONE: dignified restraint; “strength without slaughter.”
GM HOOKS: White Cloaks oppose PCs’ heist methods; later become allies if PCs spare civilians.

16) DAHAK — CATASTROPHE FIRE
ROLE: radical purge; violent correction.
ORIGIN: dragon god via elevated power; exploited by Thanat.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on fear, destruction, supremacy myths.
PROXIES: Dahakian kobold cults, drake lieutenants.
CULT TONE: terror as sermon.
GM HOOKS: Dahak’s lieutenants can be defeated; Dahak himself reshapes the battlefield as disaster.

====================================================================
E) GOZREH AND BOTTOM-UP DIVINITY
====================================================================

17) GOZREH — UNIFIED NATURE SPIRIT (PHARASMA’S CREATION)
ROLE: make nature an actor; regulate extraction by price.
ORIGIN: created/assembled by Pharasma from nature flow; a “seat” for ecology.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on primal cycles, storms, growth, fear of wilderness.
PROXIES: druids, awakened nodes, storm-spirits, animal messengers.
CULT TONE: awe + transactional respect; “take nothing for free.”
GM HOOKS: Gozreh spirit demands compensation; Asmodeus tries to legislate storms.

18) GORUM — WAR GOD EMERGENCE (BOTTOM-UP)
ROLE: war as endurance; the will to lift the sword when strength is gone.
ORIGIN: bottom-up god born from mass prayer during Great War.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on battle resolve, soldier fear overcome, communal oath.
PROXIES: war shrines, champions, battlefield spirits; rapid uplift of war-peoples.
CULT TONE: direct, honest, brutal hope.
GM HOOKS: Gorum’s priests offer courage rituals; but they also “tax” peace (peace starves them).

19) CALISTRIA — MASK, DESIRE, REVENGE (BOTTOM-UP, FROM A PEOPLE)
ROLE: personal freedom against shame politics; vengeance as selfhood.
ORIGIN: likely bottom-up emergence inside elven society as anti-stewardship virus.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on humiliation turned into agency; pleasure as rebellion; revenge as narrative correction.
PROXIES: salons, spies, duelists, courtesan-priesthood.
CULT TONE: silk over blade.
GM HOOKS: Calistrian agent offers PCs a social assassination; a scandal collapses an elf house.

20) LAMASHTU — MONSTER-MOTHER (BOTTOM-UP AMONG REJECTS)
ROLE: patron of discarded monstrous lineages abandoned by gods.
ORIGIN: bottom-up: when Rovagug “forgets” his early children, they create idols; Lamashtu crystallizes.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on fear, birth in pain, rejection, survival of the deformed.
PROXIES: midwives of monsters, beast cults, corrupt shamans.
CULT TONE: terrible nurture; “you are not wrong, you are hungry.”
GM HOOKS: Lamashtu cult offers refuge to persecuted mutants—at the cost of breeding new horrors.

21) ERI ASTIL (ERASTIL) — LAST HEARTH (BOTTOM-UP LATE)
ROLE: family, the last fence, survival community; emerges near Earthfall.
ORIGIN: bottom-up among peasants and frontier households under war + storm.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on homestead loyalty and defense.
PROXIES: hunters, village elders, oath circles.
CULT TONE: harsh kindness; “protect your own.”
GM HOOKS: Erastil faith may clash with Sarenrae mercy when refugees arrive.

22) NETHYS — MAGIC ASCENDED (THASSILONIC POLE)
ROLE: arcane truth; magic as divinity.
ORIGIN: mortal rise in Thassilon, fueled by obsessive arcane flow.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on spellcasting intensity; risks self-fracture.
PROXIES: mage cults, spell libraries, living rituals.
CULT TONE: ecstatic danger.
GM HOOKS: Nethys cult wants to crack a seam to learn its formula.

23) ARODEN — HUMAN HERO MYTH (PROTO-STAGE)
ROLE: legitimacy of human destiny; “we can stand without gods.”
ORIGIN: emerging hero-cult in Ash Alliance border legions; full ascension later.
METAPHYSICS: feeds on civic pride and imperial restoration longing.
PROXIES: legion saints, standards, oath rings.
CULT TONE: disciplined hope; “we build.”
GM HOOKS: Aroden cult could unite humans—or become the seed of tyranny.

24) NORGORBER — STARSTONE LATER (DO NOT CHANGE)
[CANON ✅] Keep canonical: mortal ascension via Starstone later.
In this era: only rumors, secret societies, proto-thieves.

====================================================================
APPENDIX H — DIVINE POWER LIMITATIONS (TECHNICAL)
====================================================================

No god may:
• Generate infinite Spark.
• Act directly in Material without proxy cost.
• Fully override free will without destabilizing planar membranes.

Divine Manifestation Costs:
• Avatar projection drains stored anima.
• Large miracles create temporary weak seams.
• Mass smiting accelerates entropy infiltration risk.

====================================================================
APPENDIX I — INTER-GOD RELATION MATRIX
====================================================================

Pharasma vs Asmodeus: Cycle vs Control.
Asmodeus vs Gozreh: Codification vs Wild Law.
Sarenrae vs Gozreh: Mercy vs Harsh Nature.
Torag vs Rovagug: Fortification vs Neglect.
Apsu vs Dahak: Restraint vs Catastrophe.
Calistria vs Elven Stewardship: Revenge vs Order.
Gorum vs All: War feeds but does not choose sides.

====================================================================
APPENDIX J — GREAT WAR ESCALATION MODEL
====================================================================

Phase 1: Ideological sanctions (trade blockades, magical embargoes).
Phase 2: Proxy conflicts (orc legions, hobgoblin campaigns).
Phase 3: Node seizures.
Phase 4: Dragon interventions.
Phase 5: Direct divine manifestations in limited capacity.
Phase 6: Entropy amplification — seams widen.
Phase 7: Earthfall threshold event.
