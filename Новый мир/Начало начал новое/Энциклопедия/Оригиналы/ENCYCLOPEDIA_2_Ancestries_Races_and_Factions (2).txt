WORLD LORE ENCYCLOPEDIA — PRE-EARTHFALL (PF2)
VOLUME II: ANCESTRIES/RACES + INTERNAL FACTIONS (NO MAPS)
Version: 2026-02-21

====================================================================
HOW TO READ THIS VOLUME
====================================================================
For each ancestry/race:
- ORIGIN: what base it comes from (created / uplifted / evolved / cross-planar)
- METAPHYSICS: how anima/Spark expresses in them
- CULTURE: the “feel” and social mechanisms
- FACTIONS: internal splits you can use in play
- CONFLICT AXES: what they fight about
- GM HOOKS: immediate adventure fuel

Note: We keep PF2 compatibility but we are not forced to include every PF2 ancestry
as a major civilization. Some exist as tiny remnants or anomalies.

====================================================================
A) CORE PEOPLES OF THE SUPERCONTINENT AGE
====================================================================

1) HUMANS [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: fast-adapting Spark-bearers; boosted by aboleth mentorship early on.
METAPHYSICS: highly “colorable” active anima; belief shifts quickly; huge fuel for bottom-up gods.
CULTURE: senates, legions, ports, guilds; rapid religious invention; heroic myth production.
FACTIONS:
- Ash Alliance Patriots (confederate pragmatists)
- Restorationists (want a new Azlant empire)
- Thassilonian Seal-Bureaucrats
- Thassilonian Rune-Lords (autonomy)
- Barbarians of the Margins (anti-city)
- Dragon-Captive Communities (trauma networks)
CONFLICT AXES: unity vs fragmentation; freedom vs contracts; survival vs ideals.
GM HOOKS: border legions worship proto-Aroden; a seal-regime controls water rights; rebels build a “free port.”

2) DWARVES [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: deep-earth humanoids; hold-builders; seam survivors.
METAPHYSICS: anima expresses as “oath-weight”; contracts have literal spiritual mass.
CULTURE: precedent law; Record of Debts/Grudges; gatekeeping holds; craft as religion.
FACTIONS:
- Hold Council Isolationists
- Forge-Colony Expansionists
- Reckoners (anti-duergar purists)
- Debt-Priests (legalists who treat debts as sacred)
- Duergar Exiles (enemy or tragic allies)
CONFLICT AXES: honor vs survival compromise; isolation vs trade; vengeance vs repair.
GM HOOKS: a human needs hold entry; dwarves demand a century-old debt; a duergar offers seam knowledge.

3) ELVES [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: Sarenrae-linked creation; surface cultivation architects.
METAPHYSICS: stable long-life anima; strong “status aura”; slow ideological drift but deep arrogance.
CULTURE: authoritarian stewardship; status trials; shame politics; “we know better.”
FACTIONS:
- High Stewards (strict caretakers)
- Gardens of Correction (soft assimilation)
- Whispering Groves (spirit listeners; dissent)
- Calistrian Cells (pleasure+revenge underground)
CONFLICT AXES: stewardship vs domination; unity vs house rivalry; mercy vs control.
GM HOOKS: elf reforms starve a village; a house sabotages another for node control; Calistrian youth stage a scandal.

4) ABOLETHS [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: ancient workforce line; freed by Ihys.
METAPHYSICS: grey anima + divine obscurity; resistant to propaganda; hard to “read.”
CULTURE: archivists of existence; continuity over individuals; slow time horizons; secrecy as survival.
FACTIONS:
- Keepers of the Current (non-intervention)
- Interveners (shape events)
- Quiet Students (vulnerable to Thanat)
- Ocean Wardens (seal-keepers)
CONFLICT AXES: preserve vs intervene; secrecy vs alliance; survival vs justice.
GM HOOKS: aboleths offer knowledge at cruel price; Azarketi demand confession; Intervener plots coup.

5) DRAGONS [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: apex creations of Rovagug; ecological crowns.
METAPHYSICS: huge anima reservoirs; mythic “presence” that warps local belief.
CULTURE: nest-right, hoard-law, dominance; some treat mortals as cattle, others as clients.
FACTIONS:
- Apsuists (pact loyalists)
- Dahakians (radicals)
- Neutral Ancients (withdrawn hoarders)
CONFLICT AXES: pact vs supremacy; survival vs coexistence; pride vs pragmatism.
GM HOOKS: Apsuist dragon hires mortals; Dahakian cult burns ports; neutral ancient trades reality-artefacts for silence.

6) KOBOLDS [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: dragon-aligned “hands” of nests; evolved into full culture.
METAPHYSICS: oath-chains; devotion converts into communal will-magic (trapcraft + ritual).
CULTURE: engineering, logistics, tunnels, traps, cult enforcement.
FACTIONS:
- Nest Engineers (builders)
- Oath Priests (cult administrators)
- Free-Kobolds (want autonomy from dragons)
CONFLICT AXES: service vs independence; survival vs ambition.
GM HOOKS: kobolds tunnel under a rune hub; kobold free-clan offers alliance against Dahak.

B) OCEANIC BLOCK
====================================================================

7) AZARKETI [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: aboleth-faithful humans reshaped for ocean life.
METAPHYSICS: memory-songs hold identity; trauma colors anima deep and slow.
CULTURE: coral memory-houses; song-law; diaspora identity.
FACTIONS:
- Surface-Returners (want land back)
- Deep Loyalists (still trust aboleths)
- Vengeance Choirs (anti-aboleth radicals)
CONFLICT AXES: loyalty vs betrayal; memory vs survival.
GM HOOKS: Azarketi discover proof of silence; attempt to seize an aboleth archive.

8) MERFOLK [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: created/uplifted by aboleths as intermediaries.
METAPHYSICS: ritual tide-binding; long-lived continuity.
CULTURE: reef dynasties; tide-singers; seal guardians.
FACTIONS:
- Archive Loyalists
- Reef Autonomists
CONFLICT AXES: preservation vs autonomy.
GM HOOKS: merfolk ask PCs to restore a cracked seal; reef dynasty sells secrets to surface.

9) ATAMARU [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: militarized deep-sea power (project lineage).
METAPHYSICS: discipline-coded anima; “pressure rites.”
CULTURE: legion discipline; conquest logic; fortress trenches.
FACTIONS:
- Legion Command
- Hunter Clans
- Entropy Sympathizers (Thanat seep)
CONFLICT AXES: security vs imperialism; discipline vs corruption.
GM HOOKS: Atamaru seize seam station; secret Thanat cult spreads “inevitability.”

C) UNDERWORLD + KNOWLEDGE PEOPLES
====================================================================

10) SHISK [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: knowledge-keepers; can be a refugee mage lineage from Thassilon that hides with dwarves.
METAPHYSICS: vow-binding; answers as sacred currency.
CULTURE: monastic archives; “one question” pact.
FACTIONS:
- Oathkeepers (strict neutrality)
- Answer-Merchants (sell knowledge)
- Prophecy-Binders (dangerously close to reality edits)
CONFLICT AXES: neutrality vs profit; truth vs stability.
GM HOOKS: dwarves invoke the One Question; Thassilon tries to kidnap a Shisk master.

11) RATFOLK [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: urban resonance awakening of rodents near dense human anima.
METAPHYSICS: networked community anima; city-rhythm intuition.
CULTURE: markets, information brokers, scavenger guilds, tunnel diplomacy.
FACTIONS:
- Market Councils
- Tunnel Scouts
- Plague-Saints (dark cult variant)
CONFLICT AXES: survival vs recognition; trade vs theft.
GM HOOKS: ratfolk broker a truce between kobolds and dwarves; tunnel plague threatens a hold.

12) JOTUNBORN (PHARASMA’S BUILDERS) [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: Pharasma’s structural engineers after aboleths cease being reliable builders.
METAPHYSICS: seam-sense; innate ability to “feel” planar pressure.
CULTURE: professional fatalism; repair rites; minimal politics.
FACTIONS:
- Seam-Menders (pure function)
- Cycle-Loyalists (punish necromancy/seam abuse)
CONFLICT AXES: intervention vs non-involvement.
GM HOOKS: a Jotunborn arrives—means reality is cracking; factions race to exploit a seam.

13) KASHRISHI (RARE) [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: Abadar-selected dwarf subline meant to help stitch rifts; later abandoned.
METAPHYSICS: horn-wards; survival tied to seam pressure.
CULTURE: station clans in high mountains/deep chasms; blind duty; severe rites.
FACTIONS:
- Last Stations (holdouts)
- Wander-Wards (seek help; often rejected)
CONFLICT AXES: extinction vs isolation; duty vs resentment.
GM HOOKS: kashrishi ask dwarves for recognition; hunters seek horns as trophies.

14) GOLOMA (RARE) [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: isolated jungle people, likely one pre-Earthfall remnant that later sits in Arcadia.
METAPHYSICS: paranoia hardened into protective rites; “eyes and webs” myths.
CULTURE: isolation; fear of gods; refuge to fugitives at a price (erasure).
FACTIONS:
- Hidden Grove (total isolation)
- Refuge Brokers (secretly trade shelter)
CONFLICT AXES: trust vs invisibility.
GM HOOKS: PCs must negotiate asylum; Goloma demand a memory or name in payment.

D) NEWLY ADDED: GORUM’S PEOPLES (ORCS, HOBGOBLINS, GOBLINS)
====================================================================

[CANON ✅] You proposed: Gorum as a bottom-up war god during the Great War,
and that he may create (or rapidly uplift) peoples as embodiments of war-aspects.

We lock the logic like this to keep conservation law:
- Gorum does NOT “print matter from nothing.”
- He concentrates war anima already flowing from the Great War,
  then uses it to uplift existing humanoid stock or shape new lineages from battlefield residues.
- These peoples emerge near war-hot zones (battlefields, siege lines, ruined forts, cursed marches).

15) GORUM — WAR-EMERGENCE PEOPLESET OVERVIEW
Gorum’s “people” are not a unified nation at birth.
They are storms of culture condensing in different theaters.

A) ORCS — “HONEST WRATH”
ORIGIN: uplifted from harsh frontier humanoids + battlefield dead-will;
        war anima crystallizes into bodies built for endurance and direct clash.
METAPHYSICS: active anima burns hot; oaths are personal; shame is weakness; fear is poison.
CULTURE: honor in direct combat; brutality without deceit; “face your enemy.”
FACTIONS:
- Iron Oath Clans (strict duel law)
- Red Hunt Bands (predatory raiders)
- Ash-Singers (ritualists who turn grief into strength)
CONFLICT AXES: honor vs survival; clan law vs ambition.
GM HOOKS: an orc clan offers alliance if PCs fight their champion; Ash-Singers guard a battlefield node.

B) HOBGOBLINS — “DISCIPLINE AND EMPIRE”
ORIGIN: uplifted from regimented human or dwarf mercenary stocks + war logistics anima;
        shaped into bodies and minds optimized for hierarchy, drill, and strategy.
METAPHYSICS: anima forms chains: rank = spiritual structure; betrayal cracks the soul.
CULTURE: legions, banners, supply doctrine; law as weapon; obedience as virtue.
FACTIONS:
- Banner-Legions (professional army)
- Steel Magistrates (law-officers)
- Shadow Quartermasters (logistics syndicates)
CONFLICT AXES: order vs cruelty; empire vs autonomy.
GM HOOKS: hobgoblin quartermasters buy a node’s harvest; a legion offers “protection” that is occupation.

C) GOBLINS — “CUNNING AND ENGINEERING”
ORIGIN: uplifted from scavenger lineages around siege camps;
        war anima crystallizes into cleverness, improvisation, and fearless chaos.
METAPHYSICS: anima flickers; risk addiction; laughter as ward; fear converted into trick.
CULTURE: trapcraft, sabotage, pyrotechnics, quartermaster tricks; “win dirty, live.”
FACTIONS:
- Sappers (siege engineers)
- Laughing Knives (assassins/prank-warriors)
- Scrap-Priests (ritual mechanics)
CONFLICT AXES: chaos vs coordination; survival vs reputation.
GM HOOKS: goblin sappers sell tunnel plans; goblin prank triggers a diplomatic war.

[GM TOOL ⚙️] Use the three as a war triangle:
Orcs = frontline shock
Hobgoblins = campaign machine
Goblins = chaos logistics and sabotage

E) DEEP HIVE (REPLACING INSECT ANCESTRIES)
====================================================================

16) DEEP HIVE [CANON ✅]
ORIGIN: natural insects near seams; magic accelerates growth and cognition.
METAPHYSICS: hive mind emerges as a resonance pattern of active anima across colony.
CULTURE: pheromone law, brood economy, expansion math.
FACTIONS:
- Brood Queens (expansionists)
- Silent Chambers (stealth colonies)
- Seambiters (planar-eaters)
CONFLICT AXES: assimilation vs annihilation; stealth vs swarm.
GM HOOKS: pre-Earthfall colonies are small; after Earthfall they bloom when everyone is distracted.

APPENDIX D — DWARVEN CULTURAL LAYERS (DETAILED)
====================================================================

Dwarven Naming Conventions:
• Clan name precedes personal name.
• Debt titles appended publicly.
• “Stonebound” indicates life-oath to hold.

Dwarven Education:
• Children memorize 50 precedents before weapon training.
• Forge apprenticeship includes legal instruction.
• Duergar history officially omitted but whispered privately.

Dwarven Emotional Framework:
• Anger is acceptable.
• Panic is shameful.
• Public forgiveness requires ritual stone-cleaving ceremony.

====================================================================
APPENDIX E — ELVEN SOCIETAL STRUCTURE (DETAILED)
====================================================================

Elven Status Trials:
• Public debate judged by three Houses.
• Aesthetic evaluation of policy presentation.
• Emotional restraint scored as virtue.

Elven Shame Weaponization:
• Public laughter used as political assassination.
• Exile is rare; social irrelevance is preferred punishment.

Elven Architectural Doctrine:
• Every building must embody philosophical principle.
• Ugly but efficient dwarven constructions are seen as moral failure.
